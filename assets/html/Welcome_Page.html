How to use the app?

Finding the perfect recipe is easy! Just follow these simple steps:

1. Click the "Recipe Finder" button.

2. Let's say you want a vegeterian dish and you have eggs and an avocado.
Simply type "avocado egg" in the research bar and select the vegeterian recipes option.

3. Hit the "Find a Recipe" button.

4. Browse the results and click "Go to Recipe" for full instructions and a printable version.

Find delicious recipes using ingredients you already have, and customize them with our filters!
